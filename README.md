This web application connects individuals and offices in need of repair and maintenance services with skilled workers and service providers. Users can post jobs under various categories such as garden services, plumbing, cleaning, painting, and repairs (including A/C, TV, radio, fridge, fan, computer, and printing machine repairs).  

Service providers can browse available jobs and apply for tasks that match their skills. The system helps organize local service work efficiently through a simple and user-friendly interface. Users can also select workers or service providers based on reviews and comments, ensuring reliable and trusted service.
